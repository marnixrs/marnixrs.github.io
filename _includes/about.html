I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Software Science at Radboud University Nijmegen, working on the project Provably Correct Policies for Uncertain Partially Observable Markov Decision Processes under the supervision of dr. Nils Jansen and prof. dr. Frits Vaandrager.

My research is at the intersection of formal methods and AI. Specific interests are planning and learning in Markov decision processes (MDPs) and variations that extend these models with uncertainty, such as robust MDPs and partially observable MDPs (POMDPs).
